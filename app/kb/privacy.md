# Privacy Policy — Key Points

Full policy: https://www.luumen.ai/privacy (last revised May 13, 2026).

## Data Collected
- Account info: name, email, role, company, team size, photo, preferences, MFA details.
- Technical data: OS/browser, device identifiers, IP addresses, usage/session data, page views.
- Billing info: collected directly by Stripe (not Apiphani).

## How Data is Used
- Provide the Service and deliver customer support.
- Product analytics to improve Luumen (PostHog).
- LLM monitoring (LangSmith).
- Anonymized/aggregated data may be used for AI model improvement (opt-out available).

## Cookies
- Used to personalize experience and analyze site usage.
- Users can accept all, reject all, or manage preferences.

## Children
- Luumen does not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## User Rights
- Access, correct, or delete data by contacting support@luumen.ai.
- GDPR and UK Data Protection rights apply where relevant.
- California CCPA rights apply where relevant.

## Data Retention
- Customer data retained for duration of account/subscription.
- Data purged when customer terminates the service.

## International Transfers
- Data may be transferred to and processed in the United States.
- EU/UK transfers handled per European Data Protection Laws.

## Contact for Privacy
- Support: support@luumen.ai
- Policy URL: https://www.luumen.ai/privacy
