## Billing & Payment Notes
- All plans billed annually. 20% savings over monthly.
- Enterprise: custom pricing — contact sales via demo form.
- Free: try free, no credit card required.
- Pro & Team: start with free trial.
- Payments processed by Stripe. Apiphani does **not** store payment card info.
- Price changes notified by email; continued use = acceptance.
- Fees are final; **no refunds** upon cancellation.
- Late payments: 1.5% monthly interest may be charged.
