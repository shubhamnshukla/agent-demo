# Luumen Overview

Luumen is an AI-powered SSH client built for infrastructure teams — SREs, sysadmins, DevOps engineers, platform engineers, and network engineers. Developed and maintained by **Apiphani, Inc.**, which has achieved 100% uptime for clients since 2023.

Core value: Luumen transforms manual processes, SOPs, runbooks, and tribal knowledge into instantly accessible AI Skills, enabling organizations to scale operational expertise company-wide.
